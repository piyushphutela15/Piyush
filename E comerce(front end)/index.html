At Phutela Agro Agency, we prioritize customer satisfaction, environmental responsibility, and community development. Phutela Agro Agency is one of the main agrochemical organisation withinside the region. The organisation affords honour sort of consulting to the farmers. We are a leading merchant, specialising in providing a high-quality selection of Pesticides, Plant Growth Regulators, Agricultural Fertilizers, Seeds, Fungicides, Herbicides, Water Soluble, and other products at competitive costs. These products are made with the utmost care, using only the highest-quality raw materials. The firm employs an agricultural consultant and extension agents who serve as a resource for farmers in their quest for answers. The company also aims to educate and teach farmers, as well as providing free soil nutrient and irrigation water testing services. Because our clients are one of the most important components of our growth, we make every effort to meet their every need.
